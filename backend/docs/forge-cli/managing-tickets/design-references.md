Give developers visual context by attaching design links, wireframes, and files to any ticket.

## Design Links

Add links from the **Design** tab of the ticket detail view:

| Platform | What Forge does |
|----------|----------------|
| **Figma** | Detects the link and pulls in the design title and thumbnail |
| **Loom** | Embeds the video with title and preview |
| **Any URL** | Stores the link as a reference |

You can attach up to 10 design links per ticket.

## AI-Generated Wireframes

When wireframe generation is enabled during ticket creation (see [Generation Options](/docs/creating-tickets/generation-options)), Forge produces an interactive wireframe showing the proposed UI.

![Hi-res Wireframe Example](/assets/2026-03-23-ticket-tags-wireframes.html)

The wireframe uses your project's actual design tokens when a repo is connected. Click it to open a large preview, or regenerate it with updated instructions.

## File Attachments

Upload images or documents for additional context:

| Supported | Formats |
|-----------|---------|
| **Images** | PNG, JPG, GIF, WebP |
| **Documents** | PDF, DOC, DOCX |

Up to 5 files per ticket. Attachments are visible in both the web app and the CLI.
