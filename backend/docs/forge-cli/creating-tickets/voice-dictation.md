Don't feel like typing? Speak your ticket description instead.

## How It Works

1. Press **S** on your keyboard or click the **Speak** button
2. Start talking — you'll see your words appear in real time as you speak
3. Press **S** again or click the button to stop

That's it. The transcribed text is inserted into the description field. A subtle beep confirms when recording starts and stops.

## Tips

- Speak naturally — pauses and punctuation are handled automatically
- You can dictate multiple times to keep adding to the description
- Edit the text afterward if anything needs tweaking
- Works best in a quiet environment

## Browser Support

Voice dictation works in Chrome, Edge, and most modern browsers — no extensions or setup needed.
