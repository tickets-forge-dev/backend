## üéØ QUALIFYING QUESTIONS

### After Demo, Ask These:

**Question 1: Usage Frequency**
```
How often do you break down PRDs? Weekly? Monthly? Per quarter?
```

**Why:** If they say "once a quarter", they won't pay $29/month. If "3x per week", they will.

---

**Question 2: Current Solution**
```
What do you use today? Manual Jira? ChatGPT? Something else?
```

**Why:** If they're happy with ChatGPT, you need to differentiate. If manual, easy sell.

---

**Question 3: Team Size**
```
How many developers are on your team?
```

**Why:** Bigger teams = more pain = higher willingness to pay. Also helps with pricing strategy later.

---

**Question 4: Budget Authority**
```
Do you have budget to buy tools like this, or would you need approval?
```

**Why:** If they need 3 layers of approval, they're not a good early customer. Find decision-makers.

---

**Question 5: Willingness to Pay**
```
If this saved you 2-4 hours per week, what would you pay per month?
$10? $30? $50? $100?
```

**Why:** Tests price sensitivity. If they say "$10", your pricing is too high. If "$100", you're undercharging.

## üìù TIPS & TRICKS

### 1. **Use Video**
Record a 30-second Loom showing the concept. Gets 3x more responses than text alone.

### 2. **Personalize First Line**
Reference their LinkedIn post, company, or recent work. Shows you're not copy-pasting.

### 3. **Ask, Don't Tell**
"Would this save you time?" beats "This will save you time."

### 4. **Lower Friction**
"5-minute demo" beats "30-minute call". Make it easy to say yes.

### 5. **Follow Up**
80% of responses come from 2nd or 3rd follow-up. Don't give up after 1 message.

### 6. **Track Everything**
Use a spreadsheet. Review daily. What's working? What's not? Iterate fast.

### 7. **Qualify Hard**
Don't waste time on "maybes". Find people who say "YES, TAKE MY MONEY" in Week 1.
